THE LEGEND OF OAKEN FYRE

By: Harper

A long time ago in a magical world between the Sea of Mor and Koad Grove, there was a stone that marked the greatest war of OAKEN FYRE and Saille Willow pixies. The name of the stone was The Stone of War!

Beside the stone was a forest of Coll Hazel trees. Inside the trees were the mythical OAKAN FYRE FAIRIES. The Oaken Fyre were the fairies who defeated the Saille Willow pixies. Ever since then, the pixies were trapped in the stone and wanted revenge.

One day the leader of Oaken Fyre was flying to the Stone of War to see if the pixies escaped. When she got there, she saw a little girl pulling up the stone. The leader shouted “NOOOO!” But it was to late. The girl pulled up the stone and the pixies were released.

The leader went back to the village of Oaken Fyre shouting “THE PIXIES ARE BACK! GET YOUR ARROWS OUT AND FIGHT!!!” A minute later, the whole village had arrows and ready to fight. Then a pixie came. Then another. And another. And then a whole army of pixies!

Oaken Fyre used arrows to defend themselves. The leader remembered that last time they defeated the pixies, they used the magical Key of Protection. If you use the key on someone, they will be stuck in the stone. They can only escape if someone lifts it up.(someone who is not trapped in the stone.)

Some fairies helped the leader try to find the key. They searched the trees, secret rooms, underground roots, and secret passageways.

They were about to give up, but then, a little girl came into the hole of the Coll Hazel tree. When she came in, the leader immediately recognized the Key of Protection the girl was holding. The leader said”if you give me the key, I will help you get back to your world.”

The girl saw the fairies fighting desperately and agreed to give the key, and gave it to them. The leader took the key and her bow and used the bow to shoot the key.

A minute later, the pixies disappeared and so did the key, but the rock appeared again.

Two fairies carried a sign that read DO NOT TOUCH and put it beside the stone.

That night, they had a feast and the girl was also there. they danced, and played games, and had fun until three o’clock in the morning.

After two days, the girl left

This is: THE LEGEND OF OAKEN FYRE

STARFALL ISLAND AND THE SPECIAL DANDELION

“COME ON” I yelled. “WERE GOING TO BE LATE”

I was at star palace on Starfall Island. Me and my friends, Sunny Liora and Stella Elliana lived in star palace because they were leaders of Starfall Island.

Sunny came down first. She was wearing what she always did. A bright yellow dress that made her look like the sun with her long golden hair and sun shaped birthmark. Me and Stella also looked like her except we were moon and star.

“Hey moon. Where is Stella?” She said.

“I don't know and don’t call me moon. My name is Luna and you clearly know it.”

As if by magic Stella appeared out of nowhere.

“Ugh! You have to stop creepily appearing out of nowhere! And how do you even do that?”

“You just have to be quite and see,” was the only answer she gave us.

“let’s just go to school already,” I grumbled

We walked to the portal as usual and jumped in. Portals were like tunnels with a sort of galaxy like colour.

“Whe!!!!!!!!!!” Sunny yelled.

At the end of the portal I jumped onto the the soft, blue landing mat. I was a bit to late to notice Stella getting up on it. I landed right on top of Stella. A second later, Sunny landed right on top of me.

“You two are squashing me like a pancake,” Stella grumbled.

“Yeah. She’s right,” I said. “Get of us already Sun.”

“Fine,” she said with a sigh. “It’s fun to squish you though,”

After we got out of our mess, we headed for our class where we studied history. Since history was made by us and our ancestors, we knew every bit of detail.

When the class FINALLY ended, Sunny, as usual, nearly fainted.

We headed to our next lesson. Physical education. Also known as P.E. to humans.

After what seemed like ten trillion years and a lot of nearly falling asleep, we finally had our free hour also known as lunch. We always used our time to check on the special dandelion of Starfall Island. The dandelion was special because it was the “powerhouse of the island” also meaning it’s the thing keeping us alive.

“Lets go see if Helen and Cici are at the base,” I said.

As we walked down the path leading to the harmony tree, I felt there was a person following us.

“Hey Star?” I said completely calm.

“Yeah?”

“Do you mind checking security for the dandelion and tree of harmony tonight? I just fell like someone is following us. Probably because I’m paranoid, but just could you?”

Before anyone could speak we all felt a sense of doom.

“Quick!” A panicked voice rang out in the tree. “Someone come! The dandelion is withering!”

I instantly recognized the voice of Cici. I ran for the invisible barrier and used the key I had to pass through.

“This is probably another prank,” Sunny grumbled. “Remember the one-HOW??!! HOW DID THIS HAPPEN??!!”

“We don’t know,” Helen whispered. “We came earlier then you guys and noticed it had withered at the edges,” she said pointing at the edges.

“Ok. This should be easy,” I muttered. “Helen, since you’re the only earth powers, check the roots and plants around for signs if anything that can help. Star, your on security. Cici, go around and check signs of people around or was around. Sun, go stabilize animals. See if any are hurt. I will stay here and secure the place.”

As soon as I finished, they all rushed of to do their job.

About after half an hour, everyone was back with information.

“Star. What happened?” I asked

“I checked all security cameras it only showed mist and shadow around a levitating person invading and cursing the dandelion to slowly wither.”

“Okay. Sun. Report.”

“My name is Sunny. Anyways, I checked all animals in the surroundings and found no trace of dead or hurt animals. They also have seemed to not notice there was a intruder. I checked all nearby elf, fairy, and gnome villages but none seems to have noticed anything.”

“Alright. Cici anything to add to this tremendous list?” I asked.

“Not unless you count not seeing footprints and no result on ice tests,” she answered a bit shaken.

“Helen. Anything else?”

“Yes. I found the dandelion is cursed by magic. The only way to heal it is to find the person, get his hair, mix it with regular antidote.”

“Great. So the only clue we have is that this mysterious person has the ability to levitate and the element of darkness and is an elf by the shape and size of that person. Star can you pull up the files of that description?”

“Okay. Mysterious elf. Element darkness. Power to levitate,” Stella muttered while pulling out the glass tablet we used to search for information. “Oh no. You guys might want to see this. There is a thousand, nine hundred, eighty seven possibilities!”

“How do we find who it is then?” Helen voice shaking in fear of what will happen.

We all looked at each other trying to think of an idea to track this person we don’t even know.

“I have an idea!” Cici said. “I heard about this of this old way to track some one. I think it is called Magic Tracking. Where you track magic and spells.”

“Oh! Right! Good idea Cici!” Sunny said excitedly.

“Let me see in my book of spells about it.” Stella said.

We all waited for her to tell us about Magic Tracking as she read about it in a a book of spells she took out of her pouch.

A few minuets later, Helen peeked over to see what was on the book and broke down completely.

“The spell takes three days and only tracks where the magic happened,” she whispered.

“Oh great!” Cici grumbled. “Back to square one.”

“And we have to do this by midnight judging by the condition of the speed of it withering,” Sunny added.

“All right!” I said. “I want everyone to do what they didi at the start! Helen, check the roots and plants around for signs if anything that can help. Star, your do security check. Cici, go around and check signs of people around. Sun, go stabilize animals. See if any are hurt. I will stay here and secure the place.”

“Got it captain bossy pants,” Sun snorted.

“She joking,” Stella mumbled loud enough for everyone to hear.

Soon, everyone was gone to do their thing I looked around, checked security, asked a few fairies and gnomes a question or two. About at ten p.m. everyone was back to report there was no difference in anything.

All of us started panicking. Without the special dandelion of Starfall Island, Starfall Island and every thing with it would disappear and turn to dust. Even Sunny started banging her head on the tree of Harmony and eventually, Cici joined her.

Right before it turned eleven, I heard footsteps of someone running. It turned out to be Jessie. Newest one to join the group of trusted people. She passed the barrier panting.

“I-heard from Sun-she came-to me-explained every thing!” She said while panting.

“DO YOU HAVE THE ANTIDOTE AND A PLAN??!!” Stella demanded. “WE HAVE LESS THAN FORTY MINUTES TO FIND THE PERSON!!!!!”

“Yes, yes I brought the antidote,” Jessie replied calmly. “And on the way here, I found a strange cloaked elf looming creepily at the lake of rainbows. It sort of had a misty and gloomy feeling.”

“THE LAKE OF RAINBOW!” I shouted. “THAT IS THE PERFECT PLACE TO HIDE AND IS NEAR! EVERYONE SUMMON THIER PET!”

At my command everyone summoned their pet. My dark power nine-tailed fox, Stella’s giant floating jellyfish, Sunny’s flaming hot horse, Cici’s ice cold phoenix, and Jessie’s pink mercat (half cat with fish tail). Helen decided to stay to keep an eye on the dandelion.

As soon as we arrived, all of us saw the elf. Cloaked and misty with shadows all around while floating above the rain bow coloured river. Just as Jessie described.

“Jessie, keep the animals calm. The rest of us are going to fight.”

“You think YOU pathetic misfit can SAVE the island? NOT TODAY! My ancestors were the ones who destroyed the other lands. You have no chance!” The elf said

“W-o-w,” Cici said loudly. “ Anyone else regret coming-EKK!!!”

At the last moment, I saw a flash of shadow. I was to late. The shadow knocked me to the ground. Despite the pain, I stood up slowly.

“SUN! STAR! Blind him with the light of sun and star. Cici, ice shield. I’m going for the hair.”

“On it,” They all said.

“On my signal. GO!”

As the elf was blinded, I lunged for him and sliced of some hair with my pocket knife.

“NOOOOOO!!!” He screamed collapsing instantly and disappeared.

“JESSIE COME WITH ME!”

As we ran toward the tree of harmony, I mixed the hair with the antidote. It had a faint silver glow.

At the tree, the last petal was about to fall. I quickly poured it over the dandelion. After a few seconds, the island radiated with warmth and glow.

“Well,” Cici said. “We saved the world. Again”

“Let’s try not to make it a daily habit,” Stella said”

“To late” I said smiling.

As long as we were together, Starfall Island would be safe.
